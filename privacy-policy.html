Privacy Policy

Claude Usage Float Extension

Data Collection

This extension does not collect or transmit personal data to third-party services.

How It Works

  1. Reads your sessionKey cookie from Claude.ai in your browser.
  2. Uses that session to call Claude's official organization and usage endpoints.
  3. Caches your organization ID, usage data, and settings locally in browser storage.

Data Storage

  • sessionKey: cached in chrome.storage.session for 5 minutes.
  • Usage data: cached in chrome.storage.local.
  • Settings: poll interval stored in chrome.storage.local.

Data Transmission

  • Requests are sent only to https://claude.ai/api/organizations*.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or third-party tracking is used.
  • No data is sold or shared with unrelated third parties.

Permissions Used

  • alarms: schedule polling.
  • storage: cache usage data and settings locally.
  • cookies: read your Claude.ai session cookie.
  • host_permissions: https://claude.ai/*: call Claude's web API endpoints.

What This Extension Cannot Access

  • Your password.
  • Your full conversation history.
  • Other websites or other extensions.

Data Retention

All data stays in your browser and is cleared when you uninstall the extension, clear browser storage, or log out of Claude.ai.

Changes to This Policy

Changes to data handling will be reflected in this page and in the source code.
